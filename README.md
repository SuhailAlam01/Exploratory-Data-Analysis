## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

This project performs a comprehensive **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to understand dataset structure, evaluate data quality, and extract meaningful business insights. The analysis follows a structured analytical workflow designed to transform raw data into actionable information for data-driven decision-making.

---

### 🗄️ Database Exploration

* Analyzed overall database structure and schema.
* Reviewed tables, columns, and relationships between datasets.
* Verified data types and ensured structural consistency.
* Evaluated dataset size, record distribution, and completeness.

---

### 📊 Data Exploration

* Generated descriptive statistics to understand data distribution.
* Identified missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
* Performed initial profiling to assess overall data quality.
* Prepared data for deeper analytical investigation.

---

### 🧩 Dimension Exploration

* Examined categorical variables to understand segment-level behavior.
* Compared performance across categories, groups, and classifications.
* Identified dominant dimensions contributing to outcomes.
* Evaluated distribution patterns across business segments.

---

### 📅 Date Exploration (Time Analysis)

* Conducted temporal analysis to evaluate trends over time.
* Identified seasonal patterns and growth fluctuations.
* Compared performance across months, quarters, and periods.
* Detected time-based anomalies and trend shifts.

---

### 📈 Measure Exploration

* Analyzed numerical metrics using aggregations and statistical measures.
* Evaluated KPIs such as totals, averages, and variations.
* Studied measure distributions to detect irregularities.
* Validated metric reliability for further analysis.

---

### ⚖️ Magnitude Analysis

* Compared contribution levels across dimensions.
* Identified high-impact categories influencing overall performance.
* Measured scale differences between entities.
* Highlighted key drivers behind dataset outcomes.

---

### 🏆 Ranking Analysis (Top N & Bottom N)

* Ranked entities based on performance metrics.
* Identified top-performing contributors.
* Detected underperforming segments requiring attention.
* Enabled performance benchmarking and comparison.

---

### 📌 Key Outcomes

✅ Improved understanding of dataset structure and quality
✅ Identified trends, patterns, and performance drivers
✅ Generated clear analytical insights through visualization
✅ Built a strong foundation for advanced analytics and reporting
